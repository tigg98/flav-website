## P0 — Fix before driving any traffic

1. **Hero phone renders BLACK for the first seconds on every page.** Confirmed on home, /creators (hero shows one flat-gray and one black phone), /verified, /recipes/high-protein. The device-frame screenshots lazy-load, so the first paint — the moment a visitor judges the site — shows empty black rectangles. Fix: `priority` + `loading="eager"` on all above-the-fold `next/image` screenshots, and give the frame a skeleton/blur placeholder (`placeholder="blur"`). This is the single biggest premium-feel killer on the site. 🔧
2. **`public/llms.txt` is stale and actively wrong** — still says "iOS and Android," "90–97% payouts," old fee tiers, plus a fabricated-sounding "12 recipes/week per user, per internal data" stat. Either ship the `[handle]` route (it's also build item #1 in our queue) or soften the copy to "coming with your Verified profile." Shipping it is the right answer — it's the growth flywheel. 🔧

Empty-state screenshots read as "dead app" — exactly what a premium competitor to TikTok can't afford. Re-shoot all marketing screenshots from a demo account seeded with a full cookbook, a planned week, real-looking earnings ($ figures that are plausible, not aspirational), and a creator profile with 12+ recipes. 🔑 (needs the seeded account; I can script the seed data)

Re-shoot after the app's fee update ships, or crop the fee line out now. Also three screenshot files are half-resolution (473×1024 vs 1206×2622): `ask-flav.webp`, `cooking-mode.webp`, `recipe-detail.webp` — cooking-mode is prominent on the homepage.
